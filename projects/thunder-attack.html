    • Game Interface

      Status Panel: Display each value Weapons: Current weapon and amount
      Fighter: Controllable flight Enemy: Shoot down enemy to get score

      Display the status of current flight.

    • Enemy Information

      Show the information of defeated enemy.

    • Weapon

      You can use more weapons with the progress of levels.

      Railgun: single bullet Green Laser: double bullet
      Red thunder: Tracking attack Detonator: Area of effect
