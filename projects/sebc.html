    Introduction

    A role playing game. Control your character to defeat enemies and experience exciting turn-based battle. Chat with NPC and experience awesome story.

    (Project in 2012 Game Programming course)

    Platform

    Microsoft Windows

    Development Tools

    • C++

      Logic Control

    • OpenGL

      Game screen rendering

    • OpenAL

      Background music and sound effect

    Demo

    • Dialogues & Questions

      Use left click or space key to continue story.

      When you hover your mouse on a NPC, left click to start a conversation and sometimes it will show a selectable question.

    • Shop

      In downtown, there is a shop where you can buy potions.

    • Equipment Menu

      Click “I” key to enter equipment menu. It will show the status of character and you can also change equipment and use potions here.

    • Battle

      When you are in outskirts, you will go into a battle if you touch a monster.

      When you are in battle, the control panel will show the status of character and the actions that you can use. When it comes to your turn, use left mouse button to choose an action.
